Search for Cities at Similar Latitudes

On a recent summertime excursion to Stockholm, Sweden and its surroundings, I was fascinated by the enduring dusk that reigned from about 11PM to 3AM. It never got so dark that one would even need a flashlight if out on a walk.

What other cities are located at such high a latitude as Stockholm, or which cities share the fairer latitude of my own Oakland, California? This tool aims to find that out.
